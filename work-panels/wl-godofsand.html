God of Sand (2016)

I was selected among undergraduate and graduate applicants to collaborate on a digital game vertical slice in a multi-disciplinary game industry simulation course. As a programmer, my responsibilities included developing UI systems, enemy AI, game state architecture, and general feedback systems in Unity while working closely with producers, design leads, and other developers.
