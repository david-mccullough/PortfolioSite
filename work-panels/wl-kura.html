Kura (2017)

Kura was created as part of a semester project assigned in the Systems Design course at UTD. Throughout the semester, I was responsible for continusouly developing a GDD and respective game prototype while recieving weekly feedback from peers.

Kura is an action platformer with roguelike alements centered around a samurai and his cursed, life-stealing Katana.
