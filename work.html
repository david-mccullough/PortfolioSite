Scanlines (2017)

A fourth-wall breaking narrative experience where an A.I. instructor teaches the player about humanity through a series of absurd training excercises. Created in 3 weeks fo A Game By It's Cover Jam 2017.


Inspired by Tyler Dori's fictional famicase cartdridge.

Sensei (2017)

The student becomes the master in this short action game prompted by the theme "Role Reversal". Created in 48 hours for Chillennium 2017.

Responsibilties included game design, programming, and art direction.

Don't Rock The Boat (2016)

A game about a cruise ship waiter on rocky waters. The player must keep the vacationeers refreshed without spilling their water! Awarded 1st Place at Chillennium 2016, the largest national student game jam at the time.

Responsibilties included game design, programming, and art/animation.

Super Galactic Star Planet Racers (2017)

A racing game wherein you must manipulate your planet's orbit around a dancing sun via a grappling tether in order to beat your opponent in collecting points. Created in 48 hours for the Fall 2016 UTD SGDA Game Jam given the theme "Loop"

Responsibilties included programming, art, and game design.

Friendly Bomb (2015)

Bones (2016)

Roguelite action where the player can choose to end a run early to permanently save loot or risk it all for bigger rewards. Features procedural dungeons and a loveable skeleton shopkeeper.

Bon Odori (2016)

A relaxing, non-violent platformer in which a forest spirit catches fireflies in order to light the lanterns hanging from a giant tree. Was nominated by ATEC faculty and showcased along other exemplary work in games during 2016 at UTD.

God Save The Queen (2016)

Asymmetric multiplayer game that pits a sniper against an incognito spy. One player must use their wit to blend in and accomplish their task while their opponent attempts to identify and terminate them before it's too late. Has been relatively well-received on itch. Modest success with 9000+ downloads.

Maybe We're Magic (2016)

No one will be stopping this magical rock tour. Turn-based funkadelic action about escaping a hot pursuit in space.

Featured on PC Gamer as one of the best free games of the week in October 2016.
